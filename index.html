A process called pyrolysis can recycle plastic waste in the abscence of oxygen to produce gasoline. There are three types pf pyrolysis: slow, flash and fast. An 8.3 pounds of plastic can produce one gallon of fuel. A super glue can be made from gasoline and styrofoam. (super glue) The later is dangerous to be set in fire. SO BE CAREFUL!! Video clip shows the steps to make pyrolysis in a simple method.

Instead of charging per hour, the cost will be $275 on weekends and evenings. The benefits are tremendous. Sheep's poop are low odor and biogradable fertilizer. As per the ordinance of the city, sheep should be kept on a minimum area of 2 acres.
